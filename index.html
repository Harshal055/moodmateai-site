Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

MoodMate AI ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mental wellness companion app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address to create your account.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send to the AI companion are stored securely in our database to provide conversation history.
  • Mood Logs: If you log your mood, we store the mood score and optional notes.
  • Profile Data: Your chosen companion name, role, language, country, and avatar preferences.
  • Push Notification Tokens: We store your device's push token to send you scheduled motivational messages.
  • Anonymous Usage: You can use the app anonymously. We create an anonymous account that does not require personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered conversational support and mood tracking.
  • To sync your chat history across sessions.
  • To send push notifications (motivational messages) if you opt in.
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription status.
  • To improve the App's functionality and user experience.

3. AI & Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Your messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes this data according to their Privacy Policy. We do not send your name or email to OpenAI.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication, database, and serverless functions. Data is stored securely with row-level security.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. They process purchase data per their Privacy Policy.
  • Expo: Push notifications are delivered via Expo's notification service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Anonymous accounts that are not linked to a Google or Apple account may have their data cleared if the app is uninstalled. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your chat history and profile within the App.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data.
  • Opt-out: You can disable push notifications in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

MoodMate AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
